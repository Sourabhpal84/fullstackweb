About Me

Started with nothing but time, ideas, and dreams.

Sourabh Pal was born in Village Ittaiya, Palia Kalan, Lakhimpur Kheri, Uttar Pradesh. While pursuing B.Tech Final Year, he is simultaneously building MAGNEETOZ from the ground up.

His story is not about shortcuts. It is about discipline, loyalty, family responsibility, and the courage to turn a food startup into a trusted brand.
